Academic Excellence

QAT & QET Assessment System

We conduct Quarterly Assessment Tests (QAT) and Quarter End Tests (QET) to ensure every student remains academically strong and well-prepared throughout the year.

Q

QAT (Quarterly Assessment Test)

These assessments help teachers identify each student's progress in core subjects and highlight areas where additional support is needed. Regular monitoring ensures no student is left behind.

Q

QET (Quarter End Test)

Comprehensive evaluations at the end of each quarter that test overall understanding and retention. These exams prepare students for the rigor of annual board examinations.

Preparation & Strategy

Targeted Revision

Special periods arranged before every test for concept-based explanations.

Practice Material

Practice worksheets and previous test patterns provided.

Concept Clarity

Focus on understanding over memorization to build confidence.

Parent Resources

Guidelines for Parents

Your active participation is key to your child's success. Here is how you can stay involved.

Result Checking

  • Announcement: Results declared at term end. Check notice board or WhatsApp group.
  • Review: Review the detailed marks sheet sent home with the student.
  • Discussion: Schedule a meeting with the class teacher for concerns.

Parent-Teacher Meeting

  • Schedule: PTMs held monthly. Dates via SMS/WhatsApp.
  • Preparation: Bring previous reports and note down questions.
  • Goal: Discuss academic progress, behavior, and home support.
